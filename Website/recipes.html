Vegan Avocado Toast

A delicious and healthy avocado toast recipe perfect for breakfast or a light snack.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices bread (any kind you like)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cherry tomatoes, chili flakes, olive oil, or hemp seeds (optional)

Instructions:

  • Toast the bread: Toast 2 slices of bread until golden and crisp.
  • Mash the avocado: In a bowl, mash 1 ripe avocado with 1 tablespoon lemon juice, a pinch of salt, and a little pepper.
  • Spread and top: Spread the mashed avocado on the toasted bread. Add any toppings you like (optional).
  • Serve: Eat right away while the toast is still warm.

Quinoa Salad

This refreshing quinoa salad is full of flavor and nutrients, great for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  • Cook the quinoa: Rinse quinoa. Boil with water for about 15 minutes until water is absorbed. Let it cool.
  • Chop veggies: Cut cucumber and tomatoes.
  • Mix everything: In a bowl, combine quinoa, cucumber, and tomatoes. Add olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  • Serve: Enjoy right away or chill for later.

Chickpea Stew

A hearty and comforting stew made with chickpeas, tomatoes, and aromatic spices.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth (or water)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • A handful of spinach or chopped parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  • Sauté the base: Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook until soft (about 5 minutes). Add garlic, cumin, and paprika. Stir for 1 minute.
  • Add main ingredients: Add chickpeas, diced tomatoes (with juice), and vegetable broth. Stir and bring to a simmer.
  • Simmer: Let it cook for 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  • Serve: Stir in spinach at the end until wilted, or sprinkle with fresh parsley before serving.
