# Organize your files in a clear folder structure to maintain readability and manageability. For Esample:

/GreenEats/
    /assets/
        /images/      (store images here)
        /css/         (style sheets here)
        /js/          (JavaScript files here)
    homepage.html        (homepage)
    about.html           (about us page)
    recipes.html         (recipes page)
    blog.html            (blog page)
    contact.html         (contact us page)
    thankyou.html        (thank you page)

This will ensure that your project is easy to navigate, both for you and anyone reviewing it.
